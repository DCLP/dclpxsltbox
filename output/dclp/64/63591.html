Introduction

Medical fragment (treatise?) (P.Tebt.Tait 42). Papyrus scrap (3 x 4,5 cm) written along the fibres; the back is blank. At first considered as belonging to the same roll as P.Tebt. 2.679 + P.Tebt.Tait 39-41 (the Tebtunis herbal), it has been subsequently regarded as coming from a different text (cf. W.J. Tait, Demotic Literature and Egyptian Society, in Life in a Multi-Cultural Society, ed. J.H. Johnson, Chicago 1992, pp. 303-10, esp. p. 309 n. 32; Fausti 1997).

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Nicola Reggiani as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous edition (W.J. Tait, P.Tebt.Tait 42).)
