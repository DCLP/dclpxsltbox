Introduction

Medical recipe (Strassburg inv. Gd. 71). Ostrakon with a bilingual prescription for a medical preparation, with six ingredients in Demotic (among them s3ntr3gis i.e. σανδαράκης) measured in staters, and four in Greek measured in drachmas.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Francesca Corazza as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous edition (P. Viereck - W. Spiegelberg, O.Stras. I 619).)
