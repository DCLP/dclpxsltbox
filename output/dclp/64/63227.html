Introduction

Prescription. The fragment (5 x 3 cm) preserves too scanty remains of a recipe to determine wheter its pupose was medical or magical. The text is written on the recto using a reed. The hand is a large and careful uncial dating back to the 1st-2nd century AD.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Luca Iori, with former contributions by Angelica Brunazzi, as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous edition (J.E. Powell, P.Harr. I 47).)
