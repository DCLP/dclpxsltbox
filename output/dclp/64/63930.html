DCLP Transcription [xml]

Introduction

Two medical recipes. The text is written against the fibers. Palimpsest: traces of a drawing underneath, possibly a head. The ingredients are listed with the respective dosage.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Anna Monte as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous editions (ed.pr. = K.A. Worp, P.Vind.Worp 20; ed.alt. = H. Harrauer - P.J. Sijpesteijn, MPER NS XIII 1).)
