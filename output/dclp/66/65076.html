Introduction

Medical fragments (P.Ant. 3.125). Numerous fragments of a papyrus codex of good quality and of unidentifiable medical content. Large, careful, sloping bookhand dated to the 6th cent. AD.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Francesca Corazza as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous edition (J.W.B. Barnes, P.Ant. III 125).)
