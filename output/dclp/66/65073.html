Introduction

Medical fragment. The small scrap (6.6 x 4 cm) belongs to a papyrus codex and preserves the remains of 6 lines. The medical text, in a bad state of preservation, possibly deals with nosology. It is written in a large sloping book hand dated to the 6th c. AD.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Francesca Corazza as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous editions (ed.pr. = J.W.B. Barns, P.Ant. III 136; corr. = F. Kudlien ap. Barns))
