DCLP Transcription [xml]

Introduction

Medical text. The papyrus fragment measures 7.7 x 7 cm and is written on the recto along the fibers. The text is damaged on all sides and preserves remains of 8 lines. The verso is blank. The manuscript is written in a rather informal sloping book hand, and could be dated to the late 5th-early 6th c. AD.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Francesca Corazza as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous editions (ed.pr. = J.W.B. Barns, P.Ant. III 137))
