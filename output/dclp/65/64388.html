Introduction

Medical prescription. The text is written against the fibres and organized in 2 columns. The recto is blank. The recipe enumerates ingredients with stimulating and analgesic properties. It shows similarities with the λυσιπόνιον, an ointment described by Paul.Aeg. 7.19.8 (CMG 2, 376.25-377.2 Heib.), which can be used in case of palsy.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Anna Monte as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous editions (ed.pr. = J.E. Powell, P.Harr. I 98; ed.alt. = A.M. Ieraci Bio, "Estratto Provvisorio del Corpus dei Ppairi Greci di Medicina", ed. I. Andorlini, Firenze 1998, pp. 63-5; corr. L.C. Youtie, ZPE 27, 1977, p. 144).)
