Introduction

Letter and medical recipes. Fragment (30 x 9 cm) of a papyrus sheet containing on the recto a letter to a physician; on verso, some prescriptions in a second hand (trans fibras) and the name of the addressee, which is written perpendicular to the main body of the text. The handwriting is datable to 5th cent. AD.
