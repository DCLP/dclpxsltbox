DCLP Transcription [xml]

Introduction

Medical text. Two fragments (fr. 1: 3.5 x 7.5 cm) from a parchment codex in a bad state of preservation. Only few words can be read in both frr. 1 and 2: they contain possible references to dietetics (fr. 1 B ll. 4f.), testicles (fr. 1 B l. 6 ), hand (fr. 1 B l. 13) and scrotum (fr. 2 A l. 8). The ink is much faded and the script, rather irregular in the shapes and sizes of letters, is similar to that of P.Ant. I 28 and could be dated to the 3rd-4th c. AD.

(This papyrus has been digitally edited by Francesca Corazza as part of the Project "DIGMEDTEXT - Online Humanities Scholarship: A Digital Medical Library based on Ancient Texts" (ERC-AdG-2013, Grant Agreement no. 339828) funded by the European Research Council at the University of Parma (Principal Investigator: Prof. Isabella Andorlini). The digital edition is mostly based on the previous editions (ed.pr. = J.W.B. Barns, P.Ant. III 133))
